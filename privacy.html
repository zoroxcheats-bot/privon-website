Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

Privon collects only the information necessary to provide secure communication:

- Email address: Used only for account creation and login verification. Never shared publicly.

- Username: Your public identity. Chosen by you. No phone numbers used.

- Messages: End-to-end encrypted. We cannot read your messages.

- Call logs: Stored locally for your reference.

2. No Phone Numbers

Privon does not collect or store phone numbers. Your identity is your username and email (email is hidden from others).

3. No Contact Upload

We do not sync or upload your contacts. You manually add people by username.

4. Data Security

All messages are end-to-end encrypted. Your data is stored securely and never sold to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Privon uses Firebase (Google) for authentication. Firebase's privacy policy applies to authentication data.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact: privon@example.com
