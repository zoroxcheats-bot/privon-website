01

Information We Collect

Privon collects only the minimum information needed to provide a secure, private messaging experience. We do not collect anything we don't need.

📧
Email Address
Used only for account creation and login. Never shown to other users. Never shared publicly.
@
Username
Your public identity on Privon. Chosen by you. This is the only thing other users can see about you.
💬
Messages
Stored securely in Firebase Firestore. Only visible to the sender and recipient. We cannot read your messages.
✨
Moments
Photos, videos, and text posts you choose to share. Auto-deleted after 24 hours. Visible only to people you specify.
02

No Phone Numbers

📵

Privon does not collect, store, or require your phone number — ever. Your identity on Privon is your username and email only. Your email is never visible to other users.

We believe your phone number is personal identifying information that messaging apps don't need. You should be able to connect with people without handing over your real-world identity.

03

No Contact Upload

Privon never accesses, syncs, or uploads your phone contacts. We do not scan your address book to find people you know.

You add friends manually by searching their @username. You are always in full control of who you connect with.

04

Moments & Stories

When you post a Moment, it is stored temporarily in our database and automatically deleted after 24 hours. The following applies:

⏱️
Auto-Expiry
All Moments are permanently deleted from our servers after 24 hours. No exceptions.
🎯
Visibility Controls
You choose exactly who sees each Moment — all friends, close friends, custom picks, or hidden from specific people.
💭
Anonymous Thoughts
Anonymous Moments do not attach your name or username to the content for viewers. Your identity is protected.
05

Data Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Messages and Moments are stored securely on Firebase infrastructure.

🔒

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for commercial purposes.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users as quickly as possible.

06

Third-Party Services

Privon uses the following third-party services to operate:

🔥
Firebase (Google)
Used for authentication, real-time database, and file storage. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Firebase. View Firebase Privacy Policy →

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or third-party data collection tools beyond what is listed above.

07

Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times:

🗑️
Delete Your Account
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings inside the app. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
✏️
Update Your Information
You can update your username at any time from Settings. Your email can be updated through Firebase authentication.
👁️
Control Your Visibility
Toggle last seen, read receipts, and per-Moment privacy controls at any time from the app's Privacy settings.
